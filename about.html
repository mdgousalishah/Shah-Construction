Our Story: Building a Legacy

Founded in 2005 by Mr. Altaf Ali Shah, Shah Construction started with a powerful vision: to bring world-class construction standards to projects across Maharashtra. What began as a small, passionate team has grown into one of the state's most trusted construction firms, known for our unwavering commitment to quality and client satisfaction.

Our portfolio is diverse, but we have established a particularly strong reputation in public infrastructure, specializing in government contracts. We excel at navigating the complexities and stringent requirements of these projects, consistently delivering high-quality results that serve the community.

This focus, combined with our private-sector work, defines our legacy of building a stronger Maharashtra.
